The humble cow does not see red; they are red-green colourblind, so do not run towards red flags as some would have you believe, but they move towards the waving objects. Whilst bulls are portrayed as being
aggressive, the cow is a social animal, preferring to herd. They are unique, and whilst the main dairy breed, the Holstein-Friesian cow is assumed to be homogenous, they each have different black and white
patterns, and are therefore as unique as a fingerprint.
